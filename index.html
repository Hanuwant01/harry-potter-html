About Harry Potter

Harry James Potter is a fictional character and the main protagonist in J.K.Rownling's *Harry Potter* series. He is known as "The Boy Who Lived" because he survived an attack from the dark wizard Lord Voldemort as a baby. He attends Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry and is sorted into the Gryffindor house .

Throughout the series, Harry becomes a hero of the wizarding world, defeating powerful dark forces and forming strong friendships that help him along the way.

Harry's Best Friends

  • Ron Weasley – Loyal, brave, and always by Harry’s side
  • Hermione Granger – Smartest witch of her age
  • Neville Longbottom – Surprising hero with a big heart
  • Luna Lovegood – Quirky and wise beyond her years
  • Hagrid – Half-giant friend and Hogwarts’ gamekeeper

Harry's Enemies

  • Lord Voldemort – The Dark Lord who tried to kill Harry as a baby
  • Draco Malfoy – Harry’s school rival from Slytherin
  • Bellatrix Lestrange – A loyal Death Eater and dangerous witch
  • Professor Umbridge – Hogwarts teacher with a cruel smile
  • Death Eaters – Followers of Voldemort who caused chaos
